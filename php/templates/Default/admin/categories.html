Categories

Here you can create Weapon-Categories for the Weapons that appear in the Battlefield-Logs. This way you are able to create your own results on the weapons-page. select(bf) will collect the data you marked for you and makes them available on the weapons page with you specified name. The only thing you have to do is adding a showing-table to your template!
